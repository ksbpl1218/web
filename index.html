Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your LEGO birthday parties special?

Our birthday parties combine hands-on building with robotics activities, creating an unforgettable kids birthday party experience. Each party includes games, challenges, robot demonstrations, and fun games that keep children engaged and excited throughout the celebration.

Do you offer children enrichment licensing?

Yes! We provide children enrichment licensing opportunities for educators and centers looking to offer our proven STEM/STEAM robotics curriculum. Our licensing program includes comprehensive training, materials, and ongoing support.

What age groups do your robotics workshops serve?

Our robotics workshops and STEM enrichment programs cater to children ages 4-14, with age-appropriate curriculum for Little Builders (4-6), Robot Explorers (7-9), and Tech Innovators (10-14).

Do you conduct workshops in schools?

Absolutely! We bring our workshops directly to schools. Our workshop in school programs can be customized for curriculum days, enrichment programs, or special events.
